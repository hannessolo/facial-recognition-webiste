Different ways to facial recognition

In practice, there are different methods for facial recognition. Historically, like PCA or feature extraction. However, in recent years (since around 2011), CNNs have started dominating the field, as they are much more accurate than previously used methods. CNNs have only become viable in recent years because of a massive increase in (1) computing power and, more importantly, (2) the amount of data available. This is becuase of how we have started to use the internet to generate and store masses of data.
